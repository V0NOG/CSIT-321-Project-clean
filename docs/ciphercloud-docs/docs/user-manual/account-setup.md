# Account Setup & Sign In

## Creating an Account

[Insert screenshot of the Sign-Up page here]

1. Open Cipher Cloud in your browser and navigate to the sign-up page (`/signup`).
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **First Name** — your given name
   - **Last Name** — your family name
   - **Email Address** — this is your login identity
   - **Password** — choose a strong password (8+ characters; mix letters, numbers, and symbols)
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. You will be redirected to the main File Manager page. Your account is ready.

:::tip Strong password tips
Use at least 12 characters. Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
:::

### Sign Up with Google

1. On the sign-up page, click **Continue with Google**.
2. Choose your Google account or enter your Google credentials when prompted.
3. You will be redirected to the main File Manager page automatically.

:::info
If you sign up with Google, you will not have a separate password for Cipher Cloud. You will always sign in using the **Continue with Google** button.
:::

## Signing In

[Insert screenshot of the Sign-In page here]

### Email and Password

1. Open Cipher Cloud and navigate to `/signin`.
2. Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**.
3. Click **Sign In**.
4. If you have two-factor authentication enabled, you will be prompted for your six-digit code.

### Google Sign-In

1. On the sign-in page, click **Continue with Google**.
2. Select your Google account.
3. You will be signed in automatically.

---

## Signing Out

1. Click your profile avatar or name in the top-right corner of the header.
2. Select **Sign Out** from the dropdown menu.
3. You will be redirected to the sign-in page.

---

## Forgotten Password

:::caution
Password reset via email is not yet available in the current version. Contact your system administrator if you are locked out of your account.
:::
